About Us

Definition: A landing page is a standalone web page designed to achieve a specific goal, typically conversion, by guiding visitors through a targeted and focused experience. It is the first point of contact after a visitor clicks on a link from an email, ad, or other digital marketing resource. A landing page is a single web page that appears in response to clicking on a search engine optimized (SEO) search result, marketing promotion, marketing email, or online advertisement. Its primary goal is to convert site visitors into sales or leads by providing a focused and targeted message, often with a clear call-to-action (CTA).
